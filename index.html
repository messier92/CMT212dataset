FOOTNOTES

This visualisation is NOT made for mobile responsiveness and should be viewed using a laptop or desktop.


The Recommended Intake values for a specific gender and age band in Part 2 are based on the author's calculations of the guidelines provided by the British Nutritional Foundation at https://www.nutrition.org.uk/attachments/article/234/Nutrition%20Requirements_Revised%20Oct%202016.pdf


The name of the burgers used in the dataset for this visualisation are obtained from the following websites on 4/5/2019:

KFC: https://www.kfc.co.uk/our-food/for-one/burgers

McDonalds: https://www.mcdonalds.com/gb/en-gb/menu/burgers.html

Burger King: https://www.burgerking.co.uk/menu/flame-grilled-burgers, https://www.burgerking.co.uk/menu/crispy-tender-chicken, https://www.burgerking.co.uk/menu/veggie-fish-salad


*"Vegetable", in this context, refers to food items such as "Vegetable Patties", "Hashbrowns", "Onion Rings", and the likes. This does NOT include common vegetables found in all burgers, such as "Lettuce" or "Tomatoes".
